Introduction

Welcome to PortHub

Engage in the darker side of the digital world and carry out cyber attacks tasked to you by various unsavoury characters, to earn Cash and XP which you can transfer to your Lil'Thug Outlaw account

Accepting Jobs

Check your Messages

People will send you job invitations regularly, which you can accept or remove from the Jobs section

Local jobs may require you to go and be in the vicinity of the network to carry out, while Remote jobs can be done from your current location but may reward less

The Map

Know your surroundings

Any jobs you accept will be marked on a real-world map around your device's location

Tap a Marker to view the job description and start the challenge

(Access to device Location will be required)

Gameplay Instructions

Don't get caught

Blitz:

Corrupt files by tapping and destroying the data packets before they reach the server. Disrupt enough of the transfer before they upload what they need.

Fallproof:

Extract the files and get out of the system. Move by destroying blocks of the same colour and rotating the view to manouver yourself. Land from above to capture the files.

Transferring Money

Get what you're given

Any Cash earned from completed jobs can be transferred to be used in your Lil' Thug Outlaw account

Different characters pay different amounts and remote jobs earn less
